Week One

The initial week we began planning on the project, we wasn't yet allocated the brief, and didn't yet have a strong idea other than the initial empathy brief. Empathy brief involved us getting into the mind-set of the potential user of the project, which was unknown to us but the only details given, was it was a technology focused fitness helper. We planned out how we can collect information about the gym, we needed to check one or more gyms out, and see the things which interested us most about it, and how it appealed, where we could improve it with technology based on both the disadvantages and advantages, the group split each gym amongst ourselves, and we worked on getting some information from the on-campus gym, which gave us a close idea of what we wanted to do. We asked for assist from the manager but he replied too late for us to get anything from him, as the empathy brief had finished. Throughout, we also worked on aspects we had control over, without the main brief for the project being known. We all discussed risks for our risk assessment
